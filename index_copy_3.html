Evolutions

Evolution or Evo for short is known to be the most difficult tournament of the year for fighting game players. It is a rare feat to win one of them, but Sonic has won six different events. Not only has it been multiple years he has won, but also multiple game titles.

Different Ganes

Sonic has dominated the Fighting Game Community (FGC) since 2013 when he competed in MK9. He didn't win an evo that year, but he did take out the champ in that game in several other tournaments. His reign of terror started in the next game Injustice where he took home an evo championship for the first time in 2014. He went on to stay the champ in the next NRS title MKX. He was evo champ in 2015 and 2016. People in the FGC started to say he could only win in NRS titles, so he jumped to a different kind of game. Dragon Ball Fighterz the biggest game of 2018 where he was still the unbeatable champ.
